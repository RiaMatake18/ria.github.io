The Art Of Dying


Learn the art of dying to Appreciate the worth of your breath. I am not the one who said we should be perfect, get out. Take a deep breath and listen to what the present says. I am not the one who said the world is perfect, get out,stop contracting these illusions. Enslaved by your imagination, surely the images are artistic.Still, the thoughts are dark.

Open the mind and let light sink. I’m not the one who said never to question it. The darkness is still alive, it is what it is, living on this 1000 years old planet. Everyone is lost in it, what they say is silence
It doesn’t even dry out the oceans. Instead it always ending with malice
Everyone always claim they got the truth.
